Admission Process

Category ‘A’ - Regular Admissions

Admissions under Category 'A' are managed by the AP State Council of Higher Education through a common entrance test EAMCET. Students prefer this institution after University Colleges due to its excellent reputation.

Category ‘B’ - Management Admissions

Admissions under Category 'B' are conducted directly by the college. These are based on the merit achieved in common entrance exams like EAMCET or AIEEE. A golden opportunity for talented students aiming for excellence.

Scheme for PIO/Foreign Nationals

We allocate 15% supernumerary seats for Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) and Foreign Nationals across both B.Tech and M.Tech programs. This ensures a diverse and international learning environment.
